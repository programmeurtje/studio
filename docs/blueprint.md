# **App Name**: Tiny Luxe Landing

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Image: Showcase a high-quality image of a tiny house to capture user attention immediately.
- Headline and Value Proposition: Clearly state the unique selling points of tiny houses, e.g., sustainability, cost-effectiveness, luxury living, etc.
- Open House Date Display: Show a list of open house dates (Friday and Saturday) from November to April.
- Contact Information: Include a contact form or direct contact details (phone, email) for inquiries.
- Image gallery: Present several high quality images in a gallery format to showcase the look and features of tiny houses
- SEO Optimization: Optimize the landing page for relevant keywords to improve search engine visibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep gold (#D4AF37) for a luxurious and elegant feel.
- Background color: Off-black (#121212) to provide a rich contrast and highlight content.
- Accent color: Bronze (#CD7F32) for highlighting interactive elements and CTAs.
- Body and headline font: 'Playfair', a serif with an elegant and high-end feel, suitable for headlines or small amounts of text. When more text is anticipated, combine Playfair with 'PT Sans' for body text.
- Use a clean, minimalist layout to emphasize luxury and spaciousness.
- Incorporate subtle, upscale icons representing key features or benefits of tiny house living.
- Introduce subtle fade-in animations as users scroll through sections, adding a layer of sophistication without being distracting.
